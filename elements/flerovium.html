Flerovium (Fl)114

Unknown Chemical Properties

More recent results show that flerovium's reaction with gold is similar to that of copernicium, showing that it is a very volatile element that may even be gaseous at standard temperature and pressure.

Symbol: Fl

Atomic mass: 289 u
