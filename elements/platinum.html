Platinum (Pt)78

Transition Metal

Platinum-cobalt, an alloy of roughly three parts platinum and one part cobalt, is used to make relatively strong permanent magnets.

Symbol: Pt

Atomic mass: 195.084 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 19.77 g/cm³

Melting: 1768.3 °C

Boiling: 3825 °C

Discovered by: Antonio de Ulloa

Year of discovery: 1735
