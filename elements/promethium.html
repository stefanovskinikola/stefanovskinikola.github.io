Promethium (Pm)61

Lanthanide

Promethium is used in atomic batteries. These batteries have a useful lifetime of about five years.

Symbol: Pm

Atomic mass: 145 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 7.26 g/cm3

Melting: 1041.85 °C

Boiling: 2999.85 °C

Discovered by: Jacob A. Marinsky, Charles D. Coryell, Lawrence E. Glendenin

Year of discovery: 1945
