Magnesium (Mg)12

Alkaline Earth Metal

The top producers of magnesium by the second decade of the 21st century included China, Russia and Turkey.

Symbol: Mg

Atomic mass: 24.305 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 1.584 g/cm3

Melting: 650 °C

Boiling: 1091 °C

Discovered by: Humphry Davy, Joseph Black

Year of discovery: 1755
