Manganese (Mn)25

Transition Metal

Manganese makes an essential element for photosynthesis. There would be no free oxygen on Earth without it.

Symbol: Mn

Atomic mass: 54.938044 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 5.95 g/cm³

Melting: 1246 °C

Boiling: 2061 °C

Discovered by: Carl Wilhelm Scheele, Johan Gottlieb Gahn, Ignatius Gottfried Kaim

Year of discovery: 1774
