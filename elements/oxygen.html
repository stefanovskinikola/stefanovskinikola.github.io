Oxygen (O)8

Reactive Nonmetal

As a gas, oxygen is clear. As a liquid, it's pale blue.

Symbol: O

Atomic mass: 15.999 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 1.141 g/cm3

Melting: -218.79 °C

Boiling: -182.962 °C

Discovered by: Antoine Lavoisier, Joseph Priestley, Carl Wilhelm Scheele

Year of discovery: 1774
