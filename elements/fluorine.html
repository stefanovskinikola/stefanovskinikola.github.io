Fluorine (F)9

Reactive Nonmetal

Initial studies on fluorine were so dangerous that several 19th-century experimenters were deemed "fluorine martyrs" after misfortunes with hydrofluoric acid.

Symbol: F

Atomic mass: 18.9984032 u

Melting: -219.62 °C

Boiling: -188.11 °C
