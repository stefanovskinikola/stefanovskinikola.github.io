Erbium (Er)68

Lanthanide

Erbia has a pink color and is used to color glass and glazes.

Symbol: Er

Atomic mass: 167.259 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 8.86 g/cm3

Melting: 1528.85 °C

Boiling: 2868 °C

Discovered by: Carl Gustaf Mosander

Year of discovery: 1843
