Strontium (Sr)38

Alkaline Earth Metal

Finely powdered strontium metal is pyrophoric, meaning that it will ignite spontaneously in air at room temperature.

Symbol: Sr

Atomic mass: 87.62 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 2.375 g/cm3

Melting: 777 °C

Boiling: 1381.85 °C

Discovered by: Humphry Davy, Adair Crawford, William Cruickshank

Year of discovery: 1790
