Lithium (Li)3

Alkali Metal

Although it is a metal, Lithium it is soft enough to cut with a knife.

Symbol: Li

Atomic mass: 6.941 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 0.512 g/cm3

Melting: 180.54 °C

Boiling: 1342 °C

Discovered by: Johan August Arfwedson

Year of discovery: 1817
