Bohrium (Bh)107

Transition Metal

The most stable known isotope of bohrium is 270Bh with a half-life of approximately 61 seconds, though the unconfirmed 278Bh may have a longer half-life of about 690 seconds.

Symbol: Bh

Atomic mass: 264 u

Year of discovery: 1981
