Chromium (Cr)24

Transition Metal

Chromium is extremely hard, and is the third hardest element behind carbon (diamond) and boron.

Symbol: Cr

Atomic mass: 51.9961 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 6.3 g/cm³

Melting: 1907 °C

Boiling: 2671.85 °C

Discovered by: Louis Nicolas Vauquelin, Martin Heinrich Klaproth

Year of discovery: 1797
