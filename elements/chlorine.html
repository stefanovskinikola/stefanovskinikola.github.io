Chlorine (Cl)17

Reactive Nonmetal

Chlorine gas, also known as bertholite, was first used as a weapon in World War I. As described by the soldiers, it had the distinctive smell of a mixture of pepper and pineapple.

Symbol: Cl

Atomic mass: 35.453 u

Melting: -101.5 °C

Boiling: -34.04 °C

Discovered by: Carl Wilhelm Scheele

Year of discovery: 1774
