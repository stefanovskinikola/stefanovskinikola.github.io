Francium (Fr)87

Alkali Metal

As little as 20–30 g (one ounce) exists at any given time throughout the Earth's crust; the other isotopes (except for francium-221) are entirely synthetic.

Symbol: Fr

Atomic mass: 223 u

Melting: 27 °C

Boiling: 676.85 °C

Year of discovery: 1939
