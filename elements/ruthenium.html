Ruthenium (Ru)44

Transition Metal

Apparently, Jedrzej Sniadecki, a Polish chemist, had produced ruthenium first but he withdrew his claim of discovery after other scientists failed to replicate his results.

Symbol: Ru

Atomic mass: 101.07 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 10.65 g/cm³

Melting: 2334 °C

Boiling: 4150 °C

Discovered by: Karl Ernst Claus

Year of discovery: 1844
