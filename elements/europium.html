Europium (Eu)63

Lanthanide

Europium becomes a superconductor when it is cooled below 1.8 K and compressed to above 80 GPa.

Symbol: Eu

Atomic mass: 151.964 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 5.13 g/cm3

Melting: 826 °C

Boiling: 1529 °C

Discovered by: Paul-Émile Lecoq de Boisbaudran, Eugène-Anatole Demarçay

Year of discovery: 1901
