Post-transition Metal

Indium makes a crackling sound when bent, similar to the “tin cry”.

Symbol: In

Atomic mass: 114.818 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 7.02 g/cm3

Melting: 156.63 °C

Boiling: 2072 °C

Discovered by: Ferdinand Reich

Year of discovery: 1863
