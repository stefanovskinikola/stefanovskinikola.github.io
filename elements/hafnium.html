Hafnium (Hf)72

Transition Metal

Most of the hafnium produced is used in the manufacture of control rods for nuclear reactors.

Symbol: Hf

Atomic mass: 178.49 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 12 g/cm³

Melting: 2227 °C

Boiling: 4601.85 °C

Discovered by: George de Hevesy, Dirk Coster

Year of discovery: 1923
