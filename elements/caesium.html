Caesium (Cs)55

Alkali Metal

Caesium is one of only five elemental metals that are liquid at or near room temperature.

Symbol: Cs

Atomic mass: 132.9054519 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 1.843 g/cm3

Melting: 28.44 °C

Boiling: 670.85 °C

Discovered by: Gustav Kirchhoff, Robert Bunsen

Year of discovery: 1860
