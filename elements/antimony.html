Antimony (Sb)51

Metalloid

Antimony(III) sulfide, Sb₂S₃, was recognized in predynastic Egypt as an eye cosmetic (kohl) as early as about 3100 BC, when the cosmetic palette was invented.

Symbol: Sb

Atomic mass: 121.76 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 6.53 g/cm3

Melting: 630.63 °C

Boiling: 1586.85 °C

Year of discovery: -1599
