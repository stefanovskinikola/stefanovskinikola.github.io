Calcium (Ca)20

Alkaline Earth Metal

Calcium is the main component of teeth and bones.

Atomic mass: 40.078 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 1.378 g/cm3

Melting: 842 °C

Boiling: 1483.85 °C

Discovered by: Humphry Davy
