Cadmium (Cd)48

Transition Metal

Cadmium compounds are used as red, orange and yellow pigments. A cadmium yellow paint was frequently used on Bob Ross' TV show The Joy of Painting.

Symbol: Cd

Atomic mass: 112.411 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 7.996 g/cm³

Melting: 321.07 °C

Boiling: 766.85 °C

Discovered by: Friedrich Stromeyer, Karl Samuel Leberecht Hermann

Year of discovery: 1817
