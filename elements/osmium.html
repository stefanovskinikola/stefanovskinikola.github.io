Osmium (Os)76

Transition Metal

Osmium tetroxide has been used in fingerprint detection and in staining fatty tissue for optical and electron microscopy.

Symbol: Os

Atomic mass: 190.23 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 20 g/cm³

Melting: 3033 °C

Boiling: 5026.85 °C

Discovered by: Smithson Tennant

Year of discovery: 1803
