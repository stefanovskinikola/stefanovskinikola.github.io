Rhenium (Re)75

Transition Metal

Rhenium is added to high-temperature superalloys that are used to make jet engine parts.

Symbol: Re

Atomic mass: 186.207 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 18.9 g/cm³

Melting: 3185 °C

Boiling: 5596.85 °C

Discovered by: Walter Noddack, Ida Noddack, Otto Berg

Year of discovery: 1925
