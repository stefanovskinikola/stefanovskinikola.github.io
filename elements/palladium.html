Palladium (Pd)46

Transition Metal

Palladium chloride was at one time prescribed as a tuberculosis treatment. This treatment had many negative side-effects, and was later replaced by more effective drugs.

Symbol: Pd

Atomic mass: 106.42 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 10.38 g/cm³

Melting: 1554.9 °C

Boiling: 2963 °C

Discovered by: William Hyde Wollaston

Year of discovery: 1803
