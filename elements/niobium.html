Niobium (Nb)41

Transition Metal

Niobium is almost always found in nature together with tantalum.

Symbol: Nb

Atomic mass: 92.90638 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 8.57 g/cm³

Melting: 2477 °C

Boiling: 4927 °C

Discovered by: Charles Hatchett

Year of discovery: 1801
