Rubidium (Rb)37

Alkali Metal

Rubidium has been used extensively in dating rocks.

Symbol: Rb

Atomic mass: 85.4678 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 1.46 g/cm3

Melting: 39.48 °C

Boiling: 688 °C

Discovered by: Gustav Kirchhoff, Robert Bunsen

Year of discovery: 1861
