Silicon (Si)14

Metalloid

Santa Clara Valley in California acquired the nickname Silicon Valley, as the element is the base material in the semiconductor industry there.

Symbol: Si

Atomic mass: 28.0855 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 2.57 g/cm3

Melting: 1410 °C

Boiling: 2355 °C

Discovered by: Antoine Lavoisier, Jöns Jacob Berzelius

Year of discovery: 1823
