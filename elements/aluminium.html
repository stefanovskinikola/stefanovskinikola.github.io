Aluminium (Al)13

Post-transition Metal

Despite its widespread occurrence in the Earth's crust, aluminium has no known function in biology.

Symbol: Al

Atomic mass: 26.9815386 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 2.375 g/cm3

Melting: 660.32 °C

Boiling: 2470 °C

Discovered by: Hans Christian Ørsted

Year of discovery: 1825
