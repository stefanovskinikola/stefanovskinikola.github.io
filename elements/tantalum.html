Tantalum (Ta)73

Transition Metal

Tantalum is widely used in making surgical instruments and implants because it resists attack by body fluids and is nonirritating.

Symbol: Ta

Atomic mass: 180.94788 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 15 g/cm³

Melting: 3019.85 °C

Boiling: 5456.85 °C

Year of discovery: 1802
