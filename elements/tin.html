Tin (Sn)50

Post-transition Metal

Tin cry is the characteristic sound heard when a bar made of tin is bent. Variously described as a “screaming” or “crackling” sound, the effect is caused by the crystal twinning in the metal.

Symbol: Sn

Atomic mass: 118.71 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 6.99 g/cm3

Melting: 231.93 °C

Boiling: 2602 °C
