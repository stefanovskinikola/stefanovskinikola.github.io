Germanium (Ge)32

Metalloid

Germanium is created by stellar nucleosynthesis. It has been detected in some of the most distant stars and in the atmosphere of Jupiter.

Symbol: Ge

Atomic mass: 72.64 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 5.60 g/cm3

Melting: 938.25 °C

Boiling: 2833 °C

Discovered by: Clemens Winkler

Year of discovery: 1886
