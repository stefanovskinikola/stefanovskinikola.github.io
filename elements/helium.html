Helium (He)2

Noble Gas

Helium was discovered on the sun before it was found on the earth.

Symbol: He

Atomic mass: 4.002602 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 0.145 g/cm³

Melting: -272.2 °C

Boiling: -268.93 °C

Discovered by: William Ramsay, Norman Lockyer, Per Teodor Cleve, Pierre Janssen

Year of discovery: 1868
