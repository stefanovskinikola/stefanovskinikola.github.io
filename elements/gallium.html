Gallium (Ga)31

Post-transition Metal

Gallium is a soft, silvery metal that melts at just above room temperature.

Symbol: Ga

Atomic mass: 69.723 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 6.095 g/cm3

Melting: 29.7646 °C

Boiling: 2400 °C

Discovered by: Paul-Émile Lecoq de Boisbaudran

Year of discovery: 1875
