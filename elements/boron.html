Boron (B)5

Metalloid

Boron is an essential plant nutrient, required primarily for maintaining the integrity of cell walls.

Symbol: B

Atomic mass: 10.811 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 2.08 g/cm3

Melting: 2076 °C

Boiling: 4000 °C

Discovered by: Humphry Davy, Joseph Louis Gay-Lussac, Louis Jacques Thénard

Year of discovery: 1808
