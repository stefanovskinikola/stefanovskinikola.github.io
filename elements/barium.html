Barium (Ba)56

Alkaline Earth Metal

Alchemists were attracted to stones made of impure barium sulfate because after exposure to light they would glow for years.

Symbol: Ba

Atomic mass: 137.327 u

Density (when liquid at melting point): 3.338 g/cm3

Melting: 727 °C

Boiling: 1897 °C

Discovered by: Humphry Davy, Carl Wilhelm Scheele

Year of discovery: 1808
